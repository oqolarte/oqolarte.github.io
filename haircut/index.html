Haircut

Published: 11 Jul 2021
  • Added sections and edited some lines in Old Computer Challenge.
  • Edited some lines in Bujo.

Today, I got a new haircut, care of Isya. We’ve been planning this for a while, but certain predicaments kept getting in the way of my having a new look.

Having a curtain as a makeshift apron over my torso as shield against falling hair, under the 30-degree-something-Celsius weather, only made me sweat more than usual. A small sacrifice to make.

It should be said that this isn’t the first time that Isya has cut my hair. I liked the result of the last time, and I like the one now.

Sulit!
