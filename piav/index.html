Skill or Virtue?

Published: 8 Mar 2021
  • Added a long-ago completed project, Imbentori.
  • Compressed the site’s logo.

After watching and rewatching videos on how to repair Apple’s Magsafe Power Adapter 1 (not sure what the official name is), I decided to actually try repairing the thing, because I need it to work: when the MacBook it came with was given to me, it had no battery. It would boot directly from the power adapter, if only there’s a working one around.

Patience is a different skill set, in my opinion. But I may be setting the bar low here for calling it a skill set. Isn’t it a Virtue?
